Son's Of Anarchy

The Son's Of Anarchy is a motorcycle club located in the fictional town of Charming, California. Also know as SAMCRO, which stands for "Son's of Anarchy Motorcycle Club, Redwood Original", were invested in a business of crime and gun running. Jackson "Jax" Teller, who is the vice president of the motorcycle club struggles to overcome obstacles presented by rival gangs, politicians, authories, and even their own residents of Charming. As a 7 Season series, it is the perfect show to watch if you enjoy crime drama, suspenseful action, and and amazing story line. I guarantee you will not be dissapointed!



Overall Review: 5/5


Would I recommend to a friend? Absolutely

Mr. Robot

Mr. Robot is a series about a drama-thriller where the main character, Elliot Alderson, who is a cyberscecurity engineer and hacker, suffers from social anxeity disorder and clinical despression. He is recruited by a revolutionary anarchist known as "Mr. Robot". He then joins a group of hacking activists called "FSOCIETY". The group's main objective is to destroy all debt records to encrypt their financial data of the biggest conglamerate in their world. This is definitely a show for someone who enjoys computers and understands some of the basics of computer science. It's extremely exhilirating series with action, drama, and suspense!



Overall Review: 4.5/5


Would I recommend to a friend? Absolutely, if they enjoy computer stuff

Vikings

Vikings is an incredible historical drama that was filmed in Ireland. Ragnar Lothbrok, the main character in this series, is one of the best-known legendary Norse heroes. Ragnar Lothbrok starts off as as a farmer who embarks on a journey which allows him to rise to the top and be crowned as a Scandinavian King. Ragnar is known for his clever wrath that gained him his notoriety. He journeyed all across England and France conquering every corner of those countries for both the aquisition of land and monetary gain. The Viking series spans for 4 completed seasons thus far, with 2 more that are going to be filmed and release in the upcoming years. Vikings is packed with non-stop action as well as an inticing story line that starts with him and continues through him as well as his family. If you enjoy history with a twist of magic, war, and drama, this is definitely the series for you to watch. I can promise you that you will not get enough of this amazing series!



Overall Review: 5/5


Would I recommend to a friend? Without a doubt
